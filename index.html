FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about HailO — from how it works to how to get started.

What can I order on HailO?+
Food, medicines, groceries, parcels, and large-scale tempo deliveries — everything you need in one place. HailO covers all your daily delivery needs through a single seamless app, so you never need to juggle multiple platforms again.
Is parcel delivery available across the whole city?+
Yes! You can send packages to any location within the city. Our parcel service includes door-to-door pickup and drop-off, live tracking, and multiple vehicle options depending on the size of your package.
Is it safe to use for medicines and prescriptions?+
Absolutely. Every pharmacy order is handled through verified partners and secure processes. You upload your prescription directly in the app, and our partners ensure all medicines are handled appropriately before delivery.
How does Tempo delivery work?+
Book a Tempo through the app or contact us directly. Choose your slot (on-demand or scheduled), tell us what needs moving, and we dispatch a tempo with crew assistance. Perfect for house shifts, office relocations, bulk B2B, furniture, and construction material.
How do I track my order in real time?+
Once confirmed, you'll see a live tracking screen in the HailO app showing your delivery partner's location and estimated arrival. You'll receive notifications at pickup, en route, and delivered.
Is there any surge pricing?+
No. We believe in fair, transparent pricing. The price you see at checkout is exactly what you pay — no surge fees, no random charges, no surprises. Ever.
What payment methods are accepted?+
HailO currently supports cash on delivery and in-app payment options. We're continuously expanding our payment methods to make checkout even more convenient.
Can I cancel an order after placing it?+
Yes, you can cancel before a driver is assigned or shortly after. Once a driver is en route, cancellations may incur a small fee. You'll be prompted to select a reason to help us improve.
How do I contact support if I have an issue?+
Via the in-app "Contact Support" feature, email at support@hailoapp.com, or call +91-XXXXXXXXXX. We're here 24/7 — day or night.
