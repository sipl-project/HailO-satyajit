Privacy Policy

How hailO collects, uses, and protects your personal information

This Privacy Policy explains how hailO collects, uses, and protects any information you provide while using our website or mobile application. This Policy is an electronic record in accordance with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and applicable rules. Your privacy is important to S V SANGHVI TECNO PRIVATE LIMITED ("hailO"). By using our Services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described here.

What Information We Collect

We may collect the following:

  • Personal details such as name and job title
  • Contact details including email address and phone number
  • Demographic information such as address, postcode, preferences, and interests
  • Transaction details, billing information, invoices, and saved preferences
  • Information collected through surveys, feedback, or promotional activities
  • Device-related data such as app install/uninstall activity

For Driver Partners, we may additionally collect: location data, profile photographs, government-issued identification (KYC), vehicle documents (registration, insurance, pollution certificate), bank account details, driving license, and fitness documentation. We may also conduct background verification checks through authorized third-party vendors.

How We Use the Information

  • Maintaining internal records and improving our Services
  • Enabling transactions and fulfilling contractual obligations
  • Managing your account, usage, and processing payments
  • Responding to queries, feedback, and support requests
  • Sending important updates regarding Services
  • Tracking service requests and delivery status
  • Conducting research and analysis
  • Sending promotional communications where permitted — you may opt out at any time

Data Security

We take appropriate security measures — physical, electronic, and managerial — to protect your information against unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure, in line with applicable Indian laws.
